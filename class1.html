Part 1: Defence, exercises, ships, missiles, and key updates

Coverage window: January to 20 August (as compiled). In IB ACIO, Current Affairs typically carries ~20 questions; defence often contributes 3-4 directly, with more embedded in GS.

Big picture

  • Exam weight: Current Affairs ~20 questions; defence alone often yields 3-4 direct questions.
  • This part covers: Defence exercises, Indian Navy ships and projects, achievements/equipment, missiles, and signature events.

Aero India and high-value tech

Aero India (Yelahanka AFS, Bengaluru)

  • Host: Yelahanka Air Force Station, Bengaluru.
  • 5th-gen aircraft displayed: F-35 (USA) and Su-57 (Russia).
  • India's status: No indigenous 5th-gen fighter in service yet; AMCA under development.

Counter-drone advances

  • Laser counter-UAS: India counted as 4th nation after the USA, China, Russia.
  • Nagastra: Loitering "suicide" munition.
  • Indrajaal: Anti-drone defence grid concept for swarm threats.

Quick check

Which base hosted Aero India in this cycle?

Aero India is traditionally hosted at Yelahanka AFS, enabling large-scale flying displays and static exhibits.

India currently operates an indigenous 5th-generation fighter.

No indigenous 5th-gen fighter is in service yet; AMCA is under development while MWF/Tejas Mk2 progress.

Fill in: India is the ____ nation to field laser counter-drone capability (after the USA, China, and Russia).

Laser DEW aids rapid, low-cost engagements against swarms, complementing kinetic counter-UAS options.

Defence exercises

India-Mongolia

  • Khan Quest: In Mongolia; Kumaon Regiment participated.
  • Nomadic Elephant: In Mongolia; 17th edition noted.

India-France

  • Shakti: Army; La Cavalerie; 8th edition.
  • Varuna: Naval, off Visakhapatnam; INS Vikrant participated.
  • La Perouse: Multinational (France-led); INS Mumbai took part.

India-Japan

  • Dharma Guardian: Army; hosted in Japan.
  • Veer Guardian: Bilateral air exercise.

India-Maldives

  • Ekuverin: Name means "friends"; recurring bilateral exercise.

India-Singapore

  • BOLD Kurukshetra: Army bilateral.
  • SIMBEX: Naval; INS Satpura (Shivalik-class), INS Delhi, INS Kiltan, and INS Shakti took part.

India-Uzbekistan

  • Dustlik: In Pune; 6th edition.

India-Bangladesh

  • Bongosagar: 6th edition naval cooperation exercise.

India-Russia

  • INDRA: 14th edition; INS Rana, INS Kuthar, and a P-8I participated.

USA and multinationals

  • Sea Dragon (Guam): Multinational ASW; Indian participation noted.
  • "Tiger" cluster: Tiger Triumph (tri-service) aligns with India-USA amphibious cooperation.
  • Desert Flag: IAF participation alongside UAE focus; MiG-29 and Jaguar fielded.

Egypt and Sri Lanka

  • Cyclone: With Egypt; hosted in Rajasthan.
  • SLINEX: With Sri Lanka; Indian participation highlighted.

High-readiness drills

  • Operational Readiness Exercise (OREx): Large maritime-centric drill with Navy, Air Force, Army, Coast Guard.
  • PASSEX with Greece: Indian participation via INS Tarkash; Greece also hosted "Iniochos" where India observed cycles.

Memory traps

  • KAZIND ↔ Kazakhstan, Khanjar ↔ Kyrgyzstan (don't swap).
  • Surya Kiran: India-Nepal; 18th edition; hosted in Nepal; Kumaon Regiment noted.

Quick check

"Ekuverin" is an exercise between India and which country?

Ekuverin literally means "friends" in Dhivehi; it's the India-Maldives bilateral exercise.

Fill in: "KAZIND" pairs India with ____, while "Khanjar" pairs India with ____.

Kazakhstan (KAZIND) vs. Kyrgyzstan (Khanjar) is a common trap—lock it in.

"Varuna" is a naval exercise between India and which nation?

India-France hold two signature exercises: Varuna (naval) and Shakti (army).

Indian Navy ships, projects, ports

Talwar-class and related batches

  • Batch 1: Talwar, Trishul, Tabar
  • Batch 2: Teg, Tarkash, Trikand
  • Batch 3: Tushil, Tamal (flagged as "last from abroad" before full indigenous ramp-up)
  • Batch 4 (India build): Triput, Tavasya (by Goa Shipyard Ltd.)
  • INS Tarkash: PASSEX with Greece; Talwar-class.

Project 17A – Nilgiri-class frigates

  • INS Nilgiri, Mahendragiri, Taragiri, Udaygiri, Dunagiri, Vindhyagiri, Hemgiri.

Project 15B – Visakhapatnam-class destroyers

  • INS Visakhapatnam, Mormugao, Imphal, Surat.

Project 75 – Kalvari (Scorpene) submarines

  • INS Kalvari, Khanderi, Karanj, Vela, Vagir, Vagsheer.

Key highlights

  • INS Varsha: New naval base coming up in Andhra Pradesh.
  • INS Arnala: First indigenous ASW shallow water craft (GRSE & L&T).
  • INS Guldar: Decommissioned; referenced for India's first underwater museum concept.
  • Vizhinjam Port (Kerala): "MSC Irina," world's largest container ship, visited.
  • Japanese CG ship Kojima: Port call at Chennai.

Quick check

Which Project corresponds to the Nilgiri-class frigates?

Project 17A follows P-17 (Shivalik) with stealthier, heavier Nilgiri-class frigates.

Fill in: ____ is the first indigenous ASW shallow water craft built with GRSE and L&T.

INS Arnala leads the ASW-SWC series enhancing littoral submarine-hunting capability.

Which destroyer class includes INS Imphal?

INS Imphal is the third ship of P-15B (Visakhapatnam-class), India's latest generation destroyers.

Defence achievements and equipment

Systems and platforms

  • Sheshnaag: Swarm drones; ~1000 km operational range figure cited in cycle.
  • Kavach: Automatic Train Protection for rail collisions (do not confuse with Kavacham: fast weather forecasting in Wayanad, Kerala).
  • SUR: ISRO's next-gen launch vehicle concept.
  • Hansa & Yashas: Indian trainer aircraft platforms.
  • eVTOL "Shunya": Urban air-taxi concept.

Procurement and infra

  • Rafale-M: Maritime variant expected for Navy by 2029 timeline.
  • MiG-21: Phasing out; known as "Flying Coffin"/NATO "Fishbed".
  • Facilities: BrahMos plant in Lucknow; Safran aircraft engine facility in Hyderabad; Volcano 155mm line in Ratnagiri.
  • Polar Research Vessel: India's first indigenous PRV with Norway collaboration (GRSE among partners).
  • COBRA Battalion (CRPF): Deployed in J&K; earlier jungle warfare focus.

Medical and soldier systems

  • Bhishma Cub: Portable medical system; gifted to Maldives.
  • Him Kavach: Extreme-cold gear enabling −60° to +20° operations.
  • Kabrodomas: Ballistic helmet referenced in cycle.
  • SanJAY: Battlefield surveillance system; HawkEye-360 sensor acquisition from USA referenced separately.

Missiles and export partners

Air defence and air-to-air

  • Akash: Exported to Armenia; Philippines also interested.
  • Astra Mk-II "Gandhiv": Air-to-air BVR missile; extended range.

Surface strike

  • Pralay: Surface-to-surface; Armenia buyer interest.
  • Club-S: Procured from Russia to enhance naval punch.

BrahMos exports

  • Philippines: Contract concluded; training ongoing.
  • Interest signalled: Vietnam and Indonesia; Armenia explored options.
  • State-of-the-art test range: New complex in Andhra Pradesh bolsters indigenous testing.

Smart traps and cross-links

  • Akash vs. Pralay: Akash is air defence (SAM); Pralay is surface-to-surface ballistic.
  • SLINEX vs. SIMBEX: SLINEX (Sri Lanka), SIMBEX (Singapore).
  • Varuna vs. Shakti: Both with France — Varuna (naval), Shakti (army).
  • Kavach vs. Kavacham: Rail ATP vs. weather forecasting (Wayanad).
  • Tiger cluster: US-linked; Tiger Triumph is tri-service amphibious cooperation with the USA.
