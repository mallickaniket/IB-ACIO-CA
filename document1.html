Introduction to IB ACIO Exam

The IB ACIO exam consists of 100 questions, with approximately 20 questions dedicated to current affairs. This section is crucial for scoring well in the examination.

Exam Tip: Current affairs questions often focus on events from the past 6-8 months, with emphasis on defense exercises, international relations, and government initiatives.
