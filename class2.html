Part 2: Sports & Awards Highlights

Streamlined coverage of major sports events, tournaments, and award ceremonies relevant for IB ACIO 2025.

Major International Sports Events

  • Asian Games 2022 (Hangzhou, held in 2023): India’s best‑ever haul — 107 medals (28 gold, 38 silver, 41 bronze).
  • FIFA Women’s World Cup 2023: Spain clinched their first title, beating England 1‑0 in the final.
  • Cricket World Cup 2023 (Men’s): Hosted by India; Australia crowned champions after defeating India.
  • World Athletics Championships 2023: Neeraj Chopra won India’s first‑ever gold in men’s javelin throw.
  • Hockey Men’s World Cup 2023: Held in Odisha; Germany beat Belgium in the final.

India’s best‑ever Asian Games medal tally was achieved in?

India’s 107 medals in Hangzhou mark its best‑ever performance in Asian Games history.

Cricket Highlights

  • WTC Final 2023: Australia defeated India at The Oval.
  • IPL 2023: Chennai Super Kings (CSK) won their 5th title, led by M.S. Dhoni.
  • Women’s U19 T20 World Cup: India beat England to win the inaugural title.
  • Asia Cup 2023: India beat Sri Lanka in the final.

Who won the inaugural ICC Women’s U19 T20 World Cup?

India’s women’s U19 team won by defeating England in the final.

Tennis & Badminton

  • Wimbledon 2023: Carlos Alcaraz won men’s singles; Markéta Vondroušová won women’s singles.
  • US Open 2023: Novak Djokovic won his 24th Grand Slam; Coco Gauff claimed her first major.
  • BWF World Championships 2023: Kunlavut Vitidsarn (men’s singles), An Se‑young (women’s singles) won titles.
  • India Open 2023: Kunlavut Vitidsarn and Akane Yamaguchi were singles champions.

Who won the 2023 US Open women’s singles title?

Coco Gauff claimed her first Grand Slam title at the 2023 US Open.

Major Sports Awards

  • Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna 2023: Awarded to Neeraj Chopra (athletics).
  • Arjuna Awardees: Includes Nikhat Zareen (boxing), Lakshya Sen (badminton), R Praggnanandhaa (chess).
  • Dronacharya Award: J Uday Kumar (kabaddi), Lalit Kumar (wrestling) among winners.

Who was awarded the Khel Ratna in 2023?

Tokyo Olympics gold medalist Neeraj Chopra received the Khel Ratna in 2023.

Records & Firsts

  • Chess: R Praggnanandhaa became the youngest Indian to reach the Chess World Cup final.
  • Football: Sunil Chhetri crossed 90 international goals, third among active male players.
  • Athletics: Parul Chaudhary won India’s first women’s 3000m steeplechase gold at Asian Games.

Who is the youngest Indian to reach a Chess World Cup final?

Praggnanandhaa’s 2023 run to the final was a landmark for Indian chess.
